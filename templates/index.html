Welcome to FoodCloud Kenya

Stop food lose and waste for the people and the planet

Our Menu Book a Table

Foodcloud Kenya aims to address the food waste problem by connecting surplus food from businesses and individuals with local charities and communities in need, thereby reducing food waste and alleviating hunger and food insecurity.

Our mission is simple: Implementing sustainable and innovative solutions that ensure surplus food reaches those in need, while also promoting long-term food security and advocating for policy changes to address the root causes of food insecurity.

  • Hunger-free Kenya, where all individuals have access to nutritious food and a future where surplus food is efficiently distributed to marginalized communities, reducing food waste..
  • With our easy-to-use platform, ordering your favorite meals is just a few clicks away.
  • Plus, with our commitment to sustainability, you can feel good knowing that every order helps support local businesses and reduces food waste.

Join us on a journey of flavor discovery and let Foodcloud Kenya bring the world of culinary delights directly to your doorstep.

Delicious Meal

Mutura: A Kenyan delicacy, Mutura is a traditional blood sausage made from a blend of minced meat, spices, and herbs, encased in a casing of intestines. Grilled to perfection, it offers a unique flavor experience, rich and savory, beloved by locals and adventurous eaters alike.

Kenyan-style Fried Fish: Fresh fish coated in a flavorful blend of spices, then fried to golden perfection. Crispy on the outside, tender and flaky on the inside, it offers a taste of coastal Kenya's culinary heritage. Served with a side of tangy dipping sauce, it's a deliciously satisfying dish that captures the essence of seaside dining.

Delicious Meal.

Kachumbari: A vibrant salad featuring diced tomatoes, onions, and cilantro, tossed in tangy lemon juice and seasoned with salt and pepper. Refreshing and flavorful, it adds a burst of freshness to any meal, complementing spicy dishes with its cool, crisp texture. A staple of Kenyan cuisine, Kachumbari is a simple yet essential side dish that delights the palate with its bright flavors and colorful presentation.
